I am an avid classical music lover, and my favorite composers are Litvinovsky, Jenkins, Albéniz, Vivaldi, Piazzolla, Mozart, Dvořák, Tchaikovsky, Mendelssohn, Schubert, Bach and Beethoven. Each of these composers has their own history and style, but what unites them is their ability to create works of breathtaking beauty.

Among my favorite pieces by Litvinovsky would be "Tales of the Magic Tree: XI. Fascinated by the Rain" which is a beautiful piece of classical music that touches me every time I hear it. From Jenkins, I recommend "Concerto Grosso for Strings "Palladio": I. Allegretto" a piece of contemporary classical music that is both moving and powerful. "Suite Española No. 1: V. Asturias - Leyenda" by Albéniz is another of my favorite pieces, it is rich in color and emotion.

From Vivaldi, I recommend "The Four Seasons" which is a classical music staple, each season is beautifully represented in this work. "Libertango" by Piazzolla is another favorite piece of mine, it is a classical tango that is both mesmerizing and passionate.

Mozart, Dvořák, Tchaikovsky, Mendelssohn, Schubert, Bach, and Beethoven are all incredibly talented composers and each of them have works that I highly recommend, such as Mozart's "Introitus. Requiem " "Symphony No. 9 "From the New World": IV" by Dvořák (a great classic), "Symphony No. 4 Finale" by Tchaikovsky, "Symphony No. 4 "Italian": IV. Saltarello. Presto" by Mendelssohn, "Presto - Prestissimo" by Schubert, "Adagio" by Bach, and "Piano Sonata No. 14 "Moonlight Sonata": III" by Beethoven.

I am also a big fan of pop/indie music, and my favorite artists are Cavetown, girl in red, Emma Peters, Angèle, Clairo and Billie Eilish.

Cavetown is a talented independent artist who has made a name for himself personal and touching music. I enjoy "Lemon Boy" and "Devil Town".

Girl in red, whose real name is Marie Ulven, is a Norwegian independent pop artist who has made a name for herself. The songs "I Wanna Be Your Girlfriend" and "summer depression" are two of my favorite songs.

Emma Peters is an independent pop artist who has made a name for hersel. I adore "Gisèle" and "Clandestina".

Angèle is a Belgian pop artist who has made a name for herself. Her songs are full of emotions and authentic feelings. The songs "La loi de Murphy" and "Perdus" are two good examples.

Clairo is an American independent pop artist who has made a name for hersel. I love "Softly" and "Sofia".

Billie Eilish is an American pop artist who has made a name for hersel. Her songs are full of emotion and genuine feelings. I appreciate "my future" and "Getting Older".

These artists are examples of my passion for pop/indie music, which is a universe rich in emotions and authentic feelings.
